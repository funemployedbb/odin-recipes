Hot Dog Mummies

Description

A fun halloween inspired meal

Ingredients

  • 8 Hot Dogs
  • 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
  • Candy Eyes (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place hot dogs in a saucepan and cover with water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until warmed through, about 5 minutes. Drain.
  3. Roll crescent dough onto a work surface; tear into 8 pieces. Roll 1 piece dough around each hot dog, creating the look of a mummy. Arrange hot dogs on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until crescent dough is golden brown and flaky, about 10 minutes.
  5. Add candy eyes and enjoy!
